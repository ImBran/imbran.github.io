Hi, I’m Brandon Marcelle

IT Professional, developer and tech writer in Southeastern Pennsylvania.

Professionally my skills include Windows Server Mangement, help-desk, development and all around problem solving.

Graduated from Pennsylvania Institute of Technology as the salutatorian in 2012 with a dual degree in Computer and Communications Technology/Systems Administration, I have been continuing my education online at CodeAcademy and Udemy in areas of iOS Application Developement, Swift, Web-Development and Javascript.

I enjoy drinking coffee and studying the complexities of the machines that we rely on everyday.
